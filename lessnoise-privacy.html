LessNoise Privacy Policy

Let it go ‚Äî literally

Effective Date: October 23, 2025

Simple Truth: LessNoise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Data Collection

LessNoise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter into the app remains exclusively on your device.

What this means:

  • Your thoughts, worries, and reflections are never sent to our servers
  • We cannot see what you write
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics, tracking, or monitoring of any kind

2. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS Core Data:

  • Local Storage: All thoughts, rituals, and preferences stored using Apple's Core Data framework
  • Encryption: Data encrypted at rest using iOS Keychain for sensitive information
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device (no iCloud, no remote servers)
  • Offline-First: App works completely in airplane mode

Data You Can Export

You have full control over your data with export options:

  • TXT Format: Plain text export of your thoughts
  • CSV Format: Spreadsheet-compatible format
  • PDF Format: Formatted document export

Exports are generated locally on your device and shared via iOS share sheet to destinations you choose.

3. In-App Purchases

LessNoise offers a one-time lifetime purchase ($14.99) for premium features:

  • Transaction Processing: Handled entirely by Apple through App Store
  • Receipt Validation: We receive an anonymized receipt token only for purchase verification
  • No Payment Data: We never see your credit card, billing information, or Apple ID
  • Privacy Compliance: All purchases subject to Apple's privacy policy

4. Third-Party Services

LessNoise does not use any third-party services, including:

  • ‚ùå No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ‚ùå No advertising networks
  • ‚ùå No crash reporting services
  • ‚ùå No social media integrations
  • ‚ùå No tracking pixels or cookies

The only external service interaction is with Apple's App Store for purchase processing.

5. Network Requests

LessNoise makes network requests only in these specific situations:

  • Purchase Validation: One-time verification with Apple's servers when you make a purchase
  • Restore Purchases: Checking with Apple to restore your previous purchase

That's it. No background requests, no data synchronization, no telemetry.

6. Release Ritual Feature

Our unique Release Ritual (fire animation for letting go of worries) is designed with ephemeral privacy:

  • Ephemeral by Design: Text you release is never stored anywhere
  • Immediate Deletion: Content discarded immediately after animation completes
  • Anonymous Stats Only: We only track the count of releases (e.g., "12 thoughts released this month"), never the content
  • True Privacy: What you release literally disappears forever

7. Children's Privacy

LessNoise is rated 4+ and appropriate for all ages:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children
  • No data collection means no special concerns for minors
  • Parents can feel confident their children's thoughts are private
  • COPPA compliant (no data collection = full compliance)

8. Data Retention

On Your Device:

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • You can delete individual thoughts anytime
  • You can export your data before deletion
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data

On Our Servers:

  • We don't have servers that store user data
  • Nothing to retain, nothing to delete

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect data, GDPR/CCPA requests are simple:

  • Right to Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Right to Export: Use our built-in export feature (TXT, CSV, PDF)
  • Right to Delete: Delete thoughts individually or uninstall app to remove all data
  • Right to Portability: Export and transfer your data anywhere

We cannot access your data even if you request it because it never leaves your device.

10. Security

We take security seriously with offline-first architecture:

  • Biometric Lock: Optional Face ID or Touch ID protection
  • Encrypted Storage: iOS Keychain for sensitive data
  • No Network Attack Surface: Offline design means no remote vulnerabilities
  • Secure Deletion: Data properly removed when deleted

11. Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change (unlikely given our offline-first design):

  • We will update this policy with a new "Effective Date"
  • Material changes will be announced in app update notes
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
