‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the free trial?
7 days from first launch. All premium features are unlocked during this time ‚Äî no credit card required.
What happens after the trial ends?
Core features (thought capture, viewing, basic sorting) remain free forever. Premium features like unlimited Release Rituals, Daily Clear-Down, and Travel Mode become limited or locked, with the option to unlock for $14.99 (one-time lifetime purchase).
Is this a subscription?
No! LessNoise is a one-time $14.99 purchase for lifetime access. No monthly fees, no recurring charges. Buy it once, own it forever.
Does the app work offline?
Yes, completely! LessNoise is offline-first by design. Everything works in airplane mode. Your purchase requires internet connection once for validation, then all features work offline forever.
Where is my data stored?
All your data stays on your device only. We use local storage (iOS Core Data) with encryption. Nothing is sent to our servers, and we cannot see what you write.
What is the Release Ritual?
Our unique fire animation for letting go of worries. Write what's weighing you down, choose an emotion, then double-tap to watch it burn away. It's cathartic, private, and surprisingly powerful. The content is never stored ‚Äî it's ephemeral by design.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Premium users can export in TXT, CSV, or PDF format. Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data, choose your format, then save or share via the iOS share sheet.
What is Travel Mode?
Tools designed specifically for travel stress: Pack & Prepare checklist, Gate Timer (counts down to boarding), Breathe Before Boarding exercise, and Post-Flight Reset. Perfect for frequent travelers or anyone who finds airports overwhelming.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open LessNoise on your new device, go to Settings ‚Üí tap "Restore Purchases." Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your premium access will be restored immediately (requires internet connection).
Does Family Sharing work?
Yes! If you enable Family Sharing for your Apple ID, your family members can access LessNoise premium features once you've purchased it.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual thoughts by swiping left ‚Üí Delete. To remove all data, uninstall the app. Since everything is stored locally on your device (not our servers), uninstalling permanently deletes all data.
Is LessNoise a therapy or mental health app?
No. LessNoise is a mental clarity and organization tool, not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing serious mental health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare provider.

üîß Troubleshooting

Purchase Issues

Purchase button not working:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your Apple ID payment method is active
  • Try restarting the app
  • If problem persists, contact Apple Support

Already purchased but showing as locked:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Tap "Restore Purchases"
  • Wait for confirmation message
  • Restart app if features don't unlock immediately

App Performance

App is slow or laggy:

  • Close other apps running in background
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in App Store
  • If you have 1000+ thoughts, consider archiving old ones

Release Ritual animation freezing:

  • This usually happens on older devices (iPhone 11 and below)
  • Try reducing animations: Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Reduce Motion
  • Close background apps to free up memory

Data & Privacy

How do I back up my data?

  • Use Settings ‚Üí Export Data ‚Üí Choose format (TXT, CSV, or PDF)
  • Save to Files app or email to yourself
  • Regular iOS backups also preserve your data

I accidentally deleted a thought:

  • Unfortunately, deletions are permanent
  • This is by design for privacy (no undo = no hidden copies)
  • We recommend exporting important thoughts regularly
